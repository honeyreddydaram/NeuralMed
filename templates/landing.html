About NeuralMed

Healthcare intelligence, built for everyone

NeuralMed is a comprehensive AI-powered health platform that combines multi-disease prediction, LLM-based clinical guidance, and a modular machine learning pipeline into a single accessible interface.

Using trained scikit-learn models, a TensorFlow CNN for medical imaging, and Google Gemini 2.0 Flash for natural language health guidance, NeuralMed brings together the best of modern AI for health education and remote care exploration.

⚠️   This platform is for educational and demo purposes only — not a substitute for professional medical advice.
